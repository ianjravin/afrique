About

Online Paradise for African Fashion

African Touch is a fashion house located in the heart of Nairobi with a set up shop at Osteria Gigiri. We specialise in authentic African Fashion from clothes to accessories and household items. Our products being handcrafted by African designers and artists who are dedicated to produce quality work..

Why Choose Us

1.Authentic

At African touch we produce authentic African designs on clothes, accessories and household items developed, designed and made by Africans since the 2000.

2.Quality

At African touch the quality of our products is top notch and embracing the rich African culture in diverse and unique designs.
